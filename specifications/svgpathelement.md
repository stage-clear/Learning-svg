# SVGPathElement

## Usage
### Simple example
```html
<svg xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg">
  <defs>
    <path d="M10 10 H 90 V 90 H 10 L 10 10" id="path"/>
  </defs>
  
  <!-- The Use get referenct<xml:href> from path -->
  <use xml:href="#path"/>
</svg>
```
